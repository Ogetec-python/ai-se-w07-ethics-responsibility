# Roadmap — Operationalizing Responsible AI (12–36 months)

## Phase 1 — Foundation (0–6 months)
- Establish roles & governance board
- Adopt the assessment checklist across teams
- Pilot policy checker on 2 projects

## Phase 2 — Integration (6–18 months)
- Integrate policy checks into CI pipelines (pre-merge)
- Add telemetry and safety metrics in production
- Run quarterly audits and publish anonymized audit reports

## Phase 3 — Maturation (18–36 months)
- Automate drift detection + retraining governance
- Expand governance to third-party models & vendors
- Develop external compliance reporting and certifications
